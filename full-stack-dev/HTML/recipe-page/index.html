Buffalo Wings

Welcome to this page providing instructions for my Buffalo Wings recipe. For reference, this includes the ingredients as well as instructions. Though you can grill or bake them, my preferred method is to smoke them using the Big Green Egg, or similar smoker grill.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in chicken wings
  • Salt
  • Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • Softened butter
  • White vinegar
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Dry chicken wings, salt both sides, and place on wire rack(s).
  2. Refrigerate rack(s) overnight.
  3. Take wire rack(s) out at least 20 minutes prior to cooking.
  4. Preheat oven/grill/smoker to 350 degree F.
  5. Add wings to oven/grill/smoker. Cook 20 minutes, flip, and repeat before removing.
  6. Add sauces, butter, vinegar, pepper, and powder ingredients to saucepan and whisk while butter melts.
  7. Toss cooked wings with sauce and serve!
